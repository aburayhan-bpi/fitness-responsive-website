    Frequenty Asked Question (FAQ)

    1. What are your gym's hours of operation?

    Our gym is open Monday through Friday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Saturday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

    2. Are personal training sessions available?

    Yes, we offer personal training sessions tailored to your fitness goals. You can book a session with one of our certified trainers through our website or by contacting us directly.

    3. Do you have facilities for special needs?

    Yes, our gym is equipped with facilities to accommodate individuals with special needs. Please contact us in advance so we can best assist you.

    4. Is there an age limit for membership?

    Members must be at least 16 years old. Those under 18 require a parent or guardian's consent.

    5. What is your cancellation policy?

    You can cancel your membership at any time with a 30-day notice. Please contact our support team for assistance with the cancellation process.

    6. Do you offer any free trials?

    Yes, we offer a one-week free trial for new members. Please contact us to arrange your free trial pass.
